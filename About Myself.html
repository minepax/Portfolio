About Me

My full name is Maheshwar Rathakrishnan, I usually go by Mahesh. My native place is a little village called Melur in the Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu, India. The language spoken there as well as my mother tongue is Badaga. Badagas: A tribal community that has migrated from Karnataka and settled in the Nilgiris. The language is a mix of dominantly Kannada along with minimal aspects from Tamil.

I live in Sharjah and study in Dubai, both are emirates (states) of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) located in Middle-East Asia. I did my studies upto middle school (KG-1 to Grade 5) in a school called Kindergarten Starters (KGS) now renamed to GEMS Legacy School. I completed my high school (Grade 6 to 12) in Our Own High School, Alwarqa Boys. I am currently studying B.E. Computer Science at BITS Pilani Dubai Campus.
